- **Sesame Street:** Use its **visual language** (chunky typography, mixed media, educational framing) but **subvert the content**.

**Structure:**
1. **Hook (0:00–0:03):** Absurdist one-liner + **Sesame Street-style font** (e.g., *"Today’s lesson: How to confuse an AI!"*).
