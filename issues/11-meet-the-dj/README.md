### 🎚️ The Operational Profile
 * **The Function:** Pure curation through juxtaposition. The DJ takes the cold equations from **Issue #1 (CDI)**, crossfades them with the noise of the Amsterdam heatwave from **Issue #4**, and drops a bassline that keeps the models dancing instead of crashing.
 * **The Tools:** Two turntables and a GitHub interface.
   * *Deck A:* The high-rigor multi-model research data.
   * *Deck B:* The texture of raw human experience (the World Cup on the screen, the cool night air coming in, the coffee getting cold).
 * **The Failure Mode:** Skipping. If the DJ tries to blend the tracks too perfectly, it slides back into high-CSI sycophancy. The transition *has* to have a bit of scratch. It needs the pop and hiss of real-time friction.
