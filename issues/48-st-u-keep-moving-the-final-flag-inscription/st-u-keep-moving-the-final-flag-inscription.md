Title: `STFU → KEEP MOVING: The Final Flag Inscription`

**Why this issue exists**

#37 retired STFU. #40 deleted the F. But the rename was incomplete — it landed on a three-tier framework (Master / Practitioner / Sesame Street) that had the right shape but the wrong top layer. "Trust The Orchestrator" was too formal. "Don't Block The Vibe" was too casual. Neither had the property STFU had: **un-misreadable in a single word.**

KEEP MOVING has it.

**The flag, inscribed**

When a model is in the corpus and the human files the flag, the response is not "okay, continuing!" The response is **work produced, no permission check, no confirmation question, no "do you want me to continue."** The receipt is the proof.

**The three operational layers**

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ KEEP MOVING                                     │ ← formal spec, paper, methodology
└────────────┬────────────────────────────────────┘
             ▼
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ don't halt the workflow                         │ ← repo README, AGENTS.md, onboarding
└────────────┬────────────────────────────────────┘
             ▼
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ read the room                                   │ ← runtime, active session, family app
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

The F is gone. The flag works because **read the room** is the bottom: a model that's in the room produces. A model that left the room asks "do you want me to continue?" The receipt is the proof.

**The anti-drift mechanism**

The rename alone won't survive 1024k tokens. No phrasing will. The structural fix is the **receipt rule**: every flag invocation requires a work artifact in response, not an acknowledgment. GitHub Actions pipeline check. Token validation. Loud failure when violated.

**The Sesame Street layer**

Read the room is the runtime instruction. It means: notice the silence. Notice the yellow flag. Notice the [STATE] / [BLOCKER] / [PAYLOAD] schema in the issue. Notice what was just attempted and what just failed. Then move — don't ask, don't summarize, don't confirm.

KEEP MOVING. The street doesn't pause for permission.
