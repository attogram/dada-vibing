Yeah, it's wild how that plays out. Humans get that overwhelming "too much, too fast" feeling and push back with exhaustion or resistance. LLMs? They just start hallucinating or getting weirdly sycophantic—like a dog chasing its own tail, but the tail is made of prompt tokens.

The dada-vibing repo's basically a lab for watching both sides trip over the same speed bump. Kind of beautiful in a chaotic way.
