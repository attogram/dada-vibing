### About Blender (Short Summary)

The **Blender Foundation** is a Dutch non-profit (Stichting) based in Amsterdam that develops and maintains **Blender**, the world’s leading open-source 3D creation suite.

- Fully open source (GPL)
- Used in film, animation, VFX, game dev, architecture, scientific visualization, and more
- Famous for productions like *Spring*, *Glass Half*, *Agent 327*, and major contributions to projects at Ubisoft, Epic, NASA, etc.
- Runs the Blender Institute (production studio) and Blender Studio (cloud platform + training)
- Strong emphasis on community, education, and unencumbered open tools
